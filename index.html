About Dr. Marcy

30+ Years of Gentle, Life-Changing Care

Dr. Marcy L. Smurthwaite has been in private chiropractic practice since 1994. A native Floridian trained at Life University in Atlanta, she uses only computerized adjusting instruments since 1987 with no manual cracking, no twisting, no popping. She was the first doctor in the Tampa Bay area to use Cold Laser Therapy, and she brings the same pioneering spirit to every patient she treats.

Life University DC CIA Certified Cold Laser Certified Spinal Rehab 100+ hrs FL Workers Comp Certified Past County Chiro President

She and her husband Robert go on missionary trips to Mexico, love fly fishing in North Carolina, and eat organic. A doctor who lives what she preaches.
