About Dr. Marcy

Care Built On Precision, Faith, And Personal Attention.

Dr. Marcy L. Smurthwaite has served Largo families since 1994, blending clinical rigor with warm one-on-one care. She designed her clinic to stay intentionally small so every treatment plan remains personal and focused.

Known for her gentle style, she specializes in Impulse iQ Chiropractic and Cold Laser Therapy for patients who want measurable relief without forceful adjustments. Her mission trips to Mexico, fly fishing in North Carolina, and organic home cooking with Robert reflect the same grounded, intentional approach she brings to practice each day.
