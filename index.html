About Dr. Marcy

Experienced care with a personal approach

Dr. Marcy L. Smurthwaite has served Largo families since 1994 through a boutique clinic model focused on one-on-one care. She is known for gentle, precise treatment that helps patients stay active without forceful adjustments.

Outside the office, she and her husband Robert enjoy missionary trips to Mexico, fly fishing in North Carolina, and cooking organic meals for family and friends. That same thoughtful energy is part of each visit.
